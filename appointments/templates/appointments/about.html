Who We Are

Beauty Experts You Can Trust

Wasafi Beauty Salon is Bomet Town's leading beauty destination, offering world-class hair, nail, skin, makeup, and spa services in a warm and welcoming environment. Our name Wasafi — meaning clean and fresh in Swahili — reflects our commitment to hygiene, quality, and excellence.

We understand that looking good is more than just appearance — it's about confidence, self-care, and feeling your absolute best. Our team of trained and passionate beauty professionals is dedicated to helping every client leave looking and feeling transformed.

From walk-in treatments to fully customized beauty packages, Wasafi offers affordable, high-quality services designed to suit every budget and style.
